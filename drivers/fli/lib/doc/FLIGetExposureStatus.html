int FLIGetExposureStatus(flicam_t cam, long *timeleft)

Find the remaining exposure time.

Documentation

Find the remaining exposure time. This functions places the remaining exposure time (in milliseconds) in the location pointed to by timeleft.

Parameters:
cam - Virtual camera to find remaining exposure time of.
timeleft - Pointer to where the remaining exposure time in msec will be placed.
Returns:
Zero on success.
Non-zero on failure.
