IRO Receives First Light!!
Below is the first light image taken during testing of the IRO in Tucson, Arizona. The image is a 10 second exposure with 3" FWHM seeing during a full moon. The image is about 10 arcminutes on a side. Click on the image for higher resolution. For more photos of the installation, see our Photo Gallery.
